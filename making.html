Main prerequisites

  • AtMega8 or better - the core of the device where all the software goes.
  • PCD8544-based display unit. These can be found in Nokia 3310, 5110 and similar, or they can be bought off ebay.
  • SN74xx280 parity generator will be necessary if you want more buttons than your AtMega has external interrupt pins.
  • Wired wheel sensor (most of them are reed switches, but anything can do).
  • AVR programmer
